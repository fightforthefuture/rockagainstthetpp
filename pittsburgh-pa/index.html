Rock Against the TPP

Pittsburgh ・ November 3 ・ 6PM-11PM

Free concert and rally to protest the Trans-Pacific Partnership at Mr Smalls Theater featuring a rare hometown performance from Anti-Flag, plus Anne Feeney, Downtown Boys, Evan Greer, Ryan Harvey, Son of Nun, Taina Asili, and more! Sign the petition & RSVP to get your FREE ticket.

You are signing this petition telling Congress to stop the TPP. Fight for the Future will send you campaign updates. You can unsubscribe at any time. Your info will be shared with Citizens Trade Campaign, Fight Back Pittsburgh, and you will be subscribed to emails from them. Ticket is required for entry but does not guarantee it. First come first serve. We suggest arriving early in case the event sells out, and to join us for a rally to stop the TPP! (Privacy Policy)
