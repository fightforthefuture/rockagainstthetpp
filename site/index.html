What can we do about it?

The TPP text has already been finalized, but before it becomes law, the U.S. Congress must approve it. It’s such a bad deal for average people that voters from across the political spectrum overwhelmingly oppose it, and more and more lawmakers are against it too. Even all the leading presidential candidates oppose it! The more people learn about the TPP, the less they like it. Rock Against the TPP is a massive effort to get out the truth about this dangerous agreement. Through a series of large-scale cultural events, protest, and trainings we’ll reach thousands of people who haven’t heard about the TPP yet, and build a movement to stop it in its tracks.
